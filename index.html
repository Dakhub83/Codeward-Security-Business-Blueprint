Questions

Before you book a call

How is this different from just using Snyk or hiring a pentest firm?+

Tooling vendors hand you findings your own engineers still have to fix. Pentest firms show up once and disappear until next year. We do both — and stay embedded between assessments, shipping the actual fix as a pull request.

Do you work with early-stage startups, or only large banks?+

Both. The engagement model and pacing differ — startups get fast, scoped sprints; established banks get longer, governance-aware roadmaps — but the same engineering team and standards apply across every segment.

What does pricing look like?+

Pilot reviews are scoped and fixed-price. Ongoing engagements (DevSecOps, vSOC, GRC) are typically retained monthly, sized to your team and surface area. Book a pilot review for a real quote, not a generic price list.

Which regions do you actually operate in?+

North America today, with regulatory-ready practices already standing in Europe (DORA, NIS2, GDPR) and Africa (NDPA, POPIA, Kenya's Data Protection Act).

Do you really do physical security too?+

Yes — IP video, access control, and alarm systems for branches and data centers, sold, installed, and kept patched. We treat that hardware the way we treat code: something with firmware that needs maintaining, not a one-time install.
